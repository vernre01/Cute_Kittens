The Rating System

What it all means

All cats are amazing! All we're doing is seeing who is the most amazing.

For every Cute vote a cat gets, it will move up our rankings one spot.

Every Not Cute rating moves a cat down one spot. So vote with care!

Cat Facts

Learn more about cats

  • While humans have 206 bones, cats have between 230-250.
  • Adult cats have 30 teeth, while kittens have 26
  • A house cat is genetically 95.6% tiger.
  • Cats can run around 48 kph (30 mph), but only over short distances.
  • Cats can jump 5 times their height
